OUR STORY

From a Vision to
Sargodha's Leading Tax Consultancy

Founded in 2021 by Mujahid Farooq, Trusted Taxation began with a simple mission: to make FBR tax services accessible, transparent, and hassle-free for the people of Sargodha and across Pakistan.

What started as a small consultancy has grown into Sargodha's most trusted name for NTN registration, income tax return filing, business registration, sales tax services, 7E certificates, 8B enabling/disabling, and FBR password recovery services. Today, we serve over 1,500+ satisfied clients from all provinces of Pakistan.

Our approach is simple: combine deep FBR expertise with genuine care for our clients. We believe that tax compliance shouldn't be complicated. That's why we handle everything remotely — you never need to visit any FBR office or wait in long queues.
